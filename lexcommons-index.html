Why LexCommons

Built for how legal
education actually works.

Not a generic LMS with a law school skin. Every decision was made with the specific reality of law school in mind.

Professor-Grounded AI
The AI doesn't just know the law — it knows what your professor thinks about the law. Uploaded syllabi and materials shape every response in LSCommons.
Honest About Limitations
CiteCommons is grounded in Indigo Book rule text and transparent about where it relies on AI training knowledge. Always be the human in the loop.
Accreditation-Native
AdminCommons was designed with ABA Standards in mind from day one — not retrofitted. Compliance documentation is a byproduct of normal use.
Designed for Finals Season
Streaks, topic mastery bars, quiz history, and a floating notes panel. Built for the reality of law school — late nights, cold calls, and outlines due Friday.
Professors Are First-Class
LSCommons gives professors their own interface — not a stripped-down teacher view. Upload materials, track engagement, and see who's preparing.
Privacy by Design
CiteCommons processes documents entirely in-browser. Nothing is stored after you close the tab. LSCommons notes stay with the student. No ad model, ever.
