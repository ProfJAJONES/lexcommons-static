What's Inside

AI as a tool, not a crutch.

Every feature reinforces critical thinking and legal reasoning. AI handles the scaffolding so you can focus on the analysis.

🧠
AI Tutoring
Ask questions and get guided explanations that help you reason through problems — not just hand you answers. Think Socratic method, powered by AI.
📝
Adaptive Quizzes
Questions that adapt to where you need practice. AI identifies gaps in your reasoning so you can build stronger analytical foundations.
📚
Course Library
Curated study materials that supplement your coursework. Organized by topic and difficulty — designed to reinforce, not replace, your reading.
📊
Progress Tracking
Honest feedback on where you stand. Track mastery by topic so you know what needs more work — no false confidence.
👨‍🏫
Professor Tools
Upload materials, guide AI-assisted study, and see how students engage. Built for professors who want AI to support learning, not undermine it.
✅
Citation Checker
Integrated with CiteCommons. Because even with AI, getting citations right still matters.
